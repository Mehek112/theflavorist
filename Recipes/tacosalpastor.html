Tacos Al Pastor

"A savory and tangy Mexican delight with marinated pork and pineapple, served in a warm tortilla."

Recipe submitted by Manasi

Ingredients

2 lbs pork shoulder, thinly sliced
3 dried guajillo chilies, seeded
2 chipotle peppers in adobo sauce
3 garlic cloves
1/2 cup pineapple juice
2 tbsp white vinegar
1 tbsp dried oregano
1 tbsp ground cumin
1 tsp paprika
Salt and pepper to taste
1 cup fresh pineapple, diced
Small corn tortillas
Chopped onions and cilantro (for garnish)
Lime wedges (for serving)

Calories

250 kcal (per taco with tortilla)
200 kcal (per 4 oz marinated pork)
50 kcal (1/4 cup pineapple)

Directions

  1. In a blender, combine guajillo chilies, chipotle peppers, garlic, pineapple juice, vinegar, oregano, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper. Blend until smooth.
  2. Marinate the sliced pork in the blended mixture for at least 4 hours or overnight in the refrigerator.
  3. Heat a grill or skillet over medium-high heat. Cook the marinated pork slices until fully cooked and slightly charred, about 5-7 minutes per side.
  4. Warm the corn tortillas on the grill or in a pan.
  5. Assemble the tacos by placing cooked pork in the tortillas. Top with diced pineapple, chopped onions, and fresh cilantro.
  6. Serve with lime wedges for a burst of citrus flavor.
