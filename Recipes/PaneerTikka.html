Paneer Tikka

"A smoky and flavorful Indian appetizer made with marinated paneer cubes grilled to perfection."

Recipe submitted by Mehek

Ingredients

250g paneer, cut into cubes
1/2 cup thick yogurt
1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
1 tsp red chili powder
1/2 tsp turmeric powder
1 tsp garam masala
1 tsp chaat masala
1 tbsp lemon juice
1 tbsp gram flour (besan)
1 tbsp mustard oil
1/2 tsp salt (adjust to taste)
1/2 cup diced capsicum and onion
Skewers for grilling

Directions

  1. In a bowl, mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, spices, lemon juice, gram flour, mustard oil, and salt.
  2. Add paneer cubes, capsicum, and onion to the marinade and mix well. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Preheat a grill or oven to medium-high heat.
  4. Thread paneer and vegetables onto skewers.
  5. Grill for 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally until slightly charred.
  6. Serve hot with mint chutney and lemon wedges.
