Guacamole

"A fresh and creamy Mexican dip made from ripe avocados, perfect for a healthy snack."

Recipe submitted by Manasi

Ingredients

3 ripe avocados
1 small onion, finely chopped
1 medium tomato, diced
2 tbsp fresh lime juice
1-2 jalapeños, finely chopped (optional)
2 tbsp fresh cilantro, chopped
Salt to taste
1 garlic clove, minced (optional)

Calories

160 kcal (per 1/2 cup serving)
240 kcal (per whole avocado)
15 kcal (1 tbsp lime juice)
20 kcal (1 small onion)
25 kcal (1 medium tomato)

Directions

  1. Cut the avocados in half, remove the pit, and scoop the flesh into a bowl.
  2. Mash the avocado with a fork until smooth or slightly chunky, based on your preference.
  3. Stir in the chopped onion, diced tomato, jalapeños (if using), lime juice, cilantro, salt, and garlic (if using).
  4. Mix well and adjust seasoning with additional lime juice or salt as needed.
  5. Serve immediately with tortilla chips, fresh vegetables, or as a topping for your favorite Mexican dishes.
