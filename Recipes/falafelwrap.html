Falafel Wrap

"A healthy and delicious vegetarian wrap filled with crispy falafel, fresh veggies, and creamy tahini sauce."

Recipe submitted by Manasi

Ingredients

1 cup dried chickpeas (soaked overnight)
1 small onion, chopped
3 garlic cloves, minced
2 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
1 tsp cumin powder
1 tsp coriander powder
Salt and pepper to taste
2 tbsp flour (or chickpea flour)
2 tbsp olive oil (for frying)
Whole wheat tortillas
1 cup lettuce, shredded
1/2 cup cucumber, sliced
1/2 cup tomatoes, diced
1/4 cup tahini sauce
Lemon wedges (for serving)

Calories

300 kcal (per wrap)
150 kcal (1 cup falafel mixture)
80 kcal (whole wheat tortilla)
70 kcal (tahini sauce, 2 tbsp)

Directions

  1. Drain and rinse the soaked chickpeas. In a food processor, blend chickpeas, onion, garlic, parsley, cumin, coriander, salt, and pepper until coarse.
  2. Stir in flour and shape the mixture into small patties or balls.
  3. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat and fry the falafel until golden brown on both sides, about 3-4 minutes per side.
  4. Warm the whole wheat tortillas in a dry pan or microwave.
  5. Assemble the wrap by placing lettuce, cucumber, and tomatoes on the tortilla. Add 3-4 falafel pieces and drizzle with tahini sauce.
  6. Roll the tortilla tightly and serve with lemon wedges on the side.
