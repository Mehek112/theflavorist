Shawarma

"A flavorful Middle Eastern dish with marinated meat, served in flatbread with fresh toppings and sauces."

Recipe submitted by Manasi

Ingredients

500g chicken or beef, thinly sliced
3 tbsp plain yogurt
2 tbsp olive oil
2 tbsp lemon juice
3 garlic cloves, minced
1 tsp paprika
1 tsp cumin
1/2 tsp turmeric
Salt and pepper to taste
4 pita breads or flatbreads
1/2 cup tahini sauce or garlic sauce
1 cup shredded lettuce
1 tomato, sliced
1 cucumber, sliced
Pickles (optional)

Calories

250 kcal (per shawarma wrap)
150 kcal (100g marinated chicken)
100 kcal (1 pita bread)
80 kcal (2 tbsp tahini sauce)

Directions

  1. In a bowl, combine yogurt, olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, paprika, cumin, turmeric, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add the sliced meat and marinate for at least 2 hours or overnight in the refrigerator.
  3. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat and cook the marinated meat until browned and fully cooked, about 8-10 minutes.
  4. Warm the pita bread and spread a layer of tahini or garlic sauce on each.
  5. Add cooked meat, lettuce, tomato, cucumber, and pickles as desired.
  6. Roll the wrap tightly, slice in half, and serve warm.
