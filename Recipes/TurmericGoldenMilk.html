Turmeric Golden Milk

"A warm and soothing drink packed with anti-inflammatory properties and immune-boosting benefits."

Recipe submitted by Mehek

Ingredients

2 cups milk (or plant-based alternative)
1 tsp turmeric powder
1/2 tsp cinnamon powder
1/4 tsp ground ginger
1/4 tsp black pepper
1 tbsp honey or maple syrup
1/2 tsp vanilla extract (optional)
1 tsp coconut oil (optional, for better absorption)

Calories

120 kcal

Directions

  1. In a saucepan, heat the milk over medium heat.
  2. Add turmeric, cinnamon, ginger, and black pepper, and whisk well.
  3. Simmer for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Remove from heat and stir in honey or maple syrup.
  5. Add vanilla extract and coconut oil if using.
  6. Pour into a cup and serve warm.
