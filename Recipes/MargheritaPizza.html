Margherita Pizza

"A classic pizza topped with fresh mozzarella, basil, and tangy tomato sauce – simple yet delightful!"

Recipe submitted by Mehek

Ingredients

1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
1/2 cup tomato sauce
1 1/2 cups fresh mozzarella cheese, shredded
1/4 cup fresh basil leaves
1 tbsp olive oil
1/2 tsp dried oregano
Salt and pepper to taste
1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, sliced
1 tbsp balsamic glaze (optional, for drizzling)

Calories

270 kcal (1 slice, average serving)
25 kcal (tomato sauce, 2 tbsp)
90 kcal (fresh mozzarella, 1/4 cup)
1 kcal (fresh basil)
120 kcal (pizza dough, 1/4 portion)
40 kcal (cherry tomatoes)
45 kcal (olive oil, 1 tbsp)

Directions

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C) and line a baking sheet or pizza stone with parchment paper.
  2. If using store-bought dough, roll it out to your desired pizza size and thickness. If using homemade dough, follow your recipe instructions.
  3. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the pizza dough, leaving a small border for the crust.
  4. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce.
  5. Scatter the sliced cherry tomatoes over the pizza.
  6. Season with salt, pepper, and dried oregano.
  7. Drizzle olive oil over the pizza and place it in the preheated oven. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  8. Once out of the oven, scatter fresh basil leaves over the pizza.
  9. Optional: Drizzle a little balsamic glaze over the pizza for added flavor and presentation.
  10. Slice and serve your Veg Margherita Pizza hot. Enjoy!
