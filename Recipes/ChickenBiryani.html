Chicken Biryani

"A flavorful and aromatic rice dish made with spiced chicken and basmati rice."

Recipe submitted by Mehek

Ingredients

2 cups basmati rice
500g chicken, cut into pieces
1 large onion, sliced
2 tomatoes, chopped
1/2 cup yogurt
1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
1 tsp turmeric powder
1 tsp red chili powder
1 tsp garam masala
1/2 cup coriander leaves
1/2 cup mint leaves
2 cups water
2 tbsp cooking oil
Salt to taste

Directions

  1. Wash and soak basmati rice for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and saut√© onions until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and chicken, cook until chicken is browned.
  4. Add tomatoes, yogurt, and spices, cook until oil separates.
  5. Add soaked rice and water, bring to a boil.
  6. Lower heat, cover, and simmer for 20 minutes.
  7. Garnish with coriander and mint leaves before serving.
