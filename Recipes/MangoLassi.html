Mango Lassi

"A refreshing and creamy mango yogurt drink, perfect for hot summer days."

Recipe submitted by Mehek

Ingredients

1 cup ripe mango pulp
1 cup plain yogurt
1/2 cup milk
2 tbsp sugar (adjust to taste)
1/2 tsp cardamom powder
Ice cubes (optional)
Chopped pistachios for garnish (optional)

Calories

99 kcal
150 kcal
42 kcal
32 kcal
3 kcal
0 kcal
15 kcal

Directions

  1. Blend mango pulp, yogurt, milk, sugar, and cardamom powder until smooth.
  2. Add ice cubes if desired and blend again.
  3. Pour into glasses and garnish with chopped pistachios.
  4. Serve chilled and enjoy!
