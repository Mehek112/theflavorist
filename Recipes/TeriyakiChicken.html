Teriyaki Chicken

"A deliciously sweet and savory grilled chicken dish, coated with homemade teriyaki sauce."

Recipe submitted by Mehek

Ingredients

4 chicken breasts (boneless and skinless)
1/4 cup soy sauce
2 tbsp honey
1 tbsp brown sugar
2 tbsp rice vinegar
1 tbsp sesame oil
2 garlic cloves, minced
1 tsp grated ginger
1 tbsp cornstarch (optional, for thicker sauce)
Sesame seeds (for garnish)
Chopped green onions (for garnish)
Salt and pepper, to taste
2 tbsp vegetable oil (for grilling)

Calories

165 kcal (per chicken breast)
20 kcal (1 tbsp soy sauce)
30 kcal (1 tbsp honey)
35 kcal (1 tbsp brown sugar)
5 kcal (1 tbsp rice vinegar)
40 kcal (1 tbsp sesame oil)
4 kcal (2 cloves garlic)
2 kcal (1 tsp ginger)
30 kcal (1 tbsp cornstarch)
10 kcal (garnishes)

Directions

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine soy sauce, honey, brown sugar, rice vinegar, sesame oil, garlic, and ginger.
  2. Bring the sauce to a simmer over medium heat, stirring occasionally. Cook for about 5-7 minutes or until the sauce thickens slightly. If you prefer a thicker sauce, mix the cornstarch with a little water and add it to the sauce. Stir well and cook for another 1-2 minutes until thickened.
  3. While the sauce is simmering, season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper.
  4. Heat vegetable oil in a grill pan or skillet over medium-high heat. Once hot, add the chicken breasts and cook for 6-7 minutes on each side, or until the chicken is fully cooked (internal temperature should reach 165°F / 75°C).
  5. During the last few minutes of cooking, brush the chicken with the teriyaki sauce, allowing it to glaze and caramelize.
  6. Once cooked, remove the chicken from the pan and let it rest for 5 minutes before slicing.
  7. Serve the sliced chicken drizzled with the remaining teriyaki sauce. Garnish with sesame seeds and chopped green onions.
  8. Enjoy your homemade Teriyaki Chicken!
