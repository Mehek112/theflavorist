Classic Cheeseburger

"A juicy and flavorful American classic, featuring a perfectly grilled beef patty with melted cheese in a toasted bun."

Recipe submitted by Manasi

Ingredients

1/2 lb ground beef
Salt and pepper to taste
2 slices cheddar cheese
2 hamburger buns
1 tbsp butter (for toasting buns)
Lettuce, tomato, and onion (for toppings)
Pickles and condiments (ketchup, mustard, mayo)

Calories

500 kcal (per serving)
250 kcal (1/2 lb ground beef)
100 kcal (2 slices cheddar cheese)
150 kcal (2 hamburger buns)

Directions

  1. Season the ground beef with salt and pepper, then form into two patties.
  2. Preheat a grill or skillet over medium-high heat and cook the patties for about 3-4 minutes per side.
  3. Place a slice of cheddar cheese on each patty and let it melt.
  4. Toast the hamburger buns with butter until golden brown.
  5. Assemble the burger by placing lettuce, tomato, and onion on the bottom bun, followed by the patty.
  6. Add pickles and condiments as desired, then top with the other bun.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy your classic cheeseburger!
