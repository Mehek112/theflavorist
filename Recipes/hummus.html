Hummus

"A creamy and healthy Middle Eastern dip made from chickpeas, tahini, lemon, and garlic."

Recipe submitted by Manasi

Ingredients

1 can (15 oz) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
3 tbsp tahini (sesame paste)
2 tbsp olive oil
2 tbsp lemon juice
2 garlic cloves, minced
1/2 tsp cumin powder
Salt to taste
Water (as needed for consistency)
Paprika and olive oil (for garnish)

Calories

150 kcal (per 1/4 cup hummus)
70 kcal (1 tbsp tahini)
40 kcal (1 tbsp olive oil)

Directions

  1. In a food processor, combine chickpeas, tahini, lemon juice, garlic, and cumin powder.
  2. Blend until smooth, adding water gradually to achieve the desired consistency.
  3. While blending, slowly drizzle in olive oil for a creamy texture.
  4. Season with salt to taste and adjust lemon juice if needed.
  5. Transfer to a bowl, drizzle with olive oil, and sprinkle with paprika before serving.
  6. Enjoy with fresh vegetables, pita bread, or as a spread.
