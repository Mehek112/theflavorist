Iced Matcha Latte

"A cool and energizing iced matcha latte, perfect for a refreshing pick-me-up on a warm day!"

Recipe submitted by Mehek

Ingredients

1 tsp matcha powder (high quality)
1/2 cup hot water (around 175°F / 80°C)
3/4 cup milk (dairy or plant-based like almond, oat, or soy milk)
1-2 tsp sweetener (honey, maple syrup, or agave, optional)
Ice cubes
1/4 tsp vanilla extract (optional for added flavor)

Calories

5 kcal (matcha powder)
50 kcal (1 cup unsweetened almond milk)
60 kcal (1 tbsp honey)
12 kcal (1/4 tsp vanilla extract)

Directions

  1. In a small bowl or cup, whisk the matcha powder with hot water until smooth and frothy. You can use a bamboo whisk (chasen) or a regular small whisk.
  2. Once the matcha mixture is ready, allow it to cool slightly or refrigerate it for a few minutes to speed up the cooling process.
  3. Fill a glass with ice cubes.
  4. Pour the cooled matcha mixture over the ice cubes.
  5. In a separate container, froth or chill your choice of milk. You can either serve it chilled or slightly warmed for a creamier texture. If using a milk frother, froth the milk until creamy.
  6. Pour the frothy milk into the glass with the matcha mixture and ice. Stir gently to combine.
  7. Sweeten the iced latte with your desired sweetener (honey, maple syrup, or agave) and stir to combine.
  8. Optionally, add a splash of vanilla extract for extra flavor.
  9. Serve immediately and enjoy your refreshing Iced Matcha Latte!
