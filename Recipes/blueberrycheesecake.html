Blueberry Cheesecake

"A creamy and luscious cheesecake topped with a sweet blueberry compote, perfect for any occasion."

Recipe submitted by Manasi

Ingredients

200g graham crackers (or digestive biscuits)
100g unsalted butter, melted
500g cream cheese, softened
200g powdered sugar
1 tsp vanilla extract
250ml heavy cream, whipped
150g fresh or frozen blueberries
2 tbsp sugar (for blueberry compote)
1 tbsp lemon juice

Calories

320 kcal (per slice)
80 kcal (per 100g blueberries)
150 kcal (per 100g cream cheese)
70 kcal (per 2 tbsp sugar)

Directions

  1. Crush the graham crackers into fine crumbs and mix with melted butter. Press the mixture into the base of a springform pan and refrigerate for 30 minutes.
  2. In a bowl, beat the cream cheese, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract until smooth and creamy.
  3. Gently fold in the whipped cream until well combined. Spread the mixture evenly over the chilled crust and refrigerate for at least 4 hours or until set.
  4. For the compote, combine blueberries, sugar, and lemon juice in a saucepan. Cook over medium heat until the blueberries break down and the sauce thickens. Let it cool.
  5. Once the cheesecake is set, pour the blueberry compote on top before serving. Slice and enjoy!
